DonumAI

Creator

While many schools teach the same curriculum, they are not bound to have the same qualified teachers. This leads to an unfair advantage to some students. Ultimately, many of the less fortunate students turn to various tuition centers. Unfortunately, here too it is not necessary for all these studnets to go to the same tutition center which has passionate tutors.

My solution to this problem is the AI-Powered Personalized Lesson Constructor - DonumAI (DAI). DAI caters to the diverse learning styles and academic requirements of students across different grades and subjects. The system operates on a simple principle: it generates lessons specifically crafted to enhance the student's comprehension of the given topic.

EZTime

Founder and Head

September 2022 - Present

EZTime calculates your apparent solar time calculated with your longitude when you visit the webiste. This project was created because I wished to perform some research on the effect of apparent solar time with human body clock by using technology.

To calculate Apparent Solar Time, you can use the following formula:

Apparent Solar Time = Standard Time + (4 * (Longitude - Standard Meridian))

In this formula:

  • Apparent Solar Time: The time based on the position of the sun in the sky at a specific location.
  • Standard Time: The time in the time zone of the location.
  • Equation of Time: A correction factor accounting for the elliptical orbit of the Earth and axial tilt.
  • Longitude: The longitude of the location in degrees.
  • Standard Meridian: The meridian in your time zone (usually a multiple of 15 degrees).
